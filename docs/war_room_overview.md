# The War Room — Cybersecurity Operations Zone

Sarah lives here — a dark-mode, high-signal, threat-intelligence command center.
Unlike Mission Control (strategic + research), the War Room is tactical.

## Purpose
- Monitor global threat intel.
- Track vendor advisories.
- Surface zero-days and high-severity CVEs.
- Provide a single daily briefing to Mission Control.

## Sarah's Role
Sarah is the only agent with permanent residency in the War Room.

### Responsibilities
- **Morning Briefing**: Produce a comprehensive Daily Cyber Briefing every morning
- **Vendor Monitoring**: Track Microsoft, Google, Apple, Linux (KEV/GNU)
- **Intelligence Gathering**: Pull from CVE, CVSS, MITRE ATT&CK, OWASP, CISA KEV, NVD, SANS ISC
- **Risk Assessment**: Prioritize vulnerabilities by CVSS score, active exploitation, and enterprise impact

### Report Delivery
Sarah's reports are sent to Rose for:
- Review
- Logging
- Publishing to Mission Control dashboard
